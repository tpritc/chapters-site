Really Fast

From exporting your podcast out of Logic to having an MP3 file with chapter markers ready to post, Chapters is the fastest way to get your podcast in the hands of your fans.


Dead Simple

No complex export settings, no pages of settings, and the shortcuts you already know. You import your file, add the chapters, then export it. It just works.

Listen Back

If you're not sure where to drop your chapter markers, listen back in Chapters. Adding a new chapter marker will drop it automatically at your current position.


Native to the Mac

No uploading, using a clunky web interface, and re-downloading. Chapters is designed for the Mac you use and love, by someone who uses and loves it too.
